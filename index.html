2025 Meta Policy Shift — Read First

Meta now classifies health & wellness as a sensitive category. Conversion tracking (Purchase, Add to Cart) is restricted — shift to Lead, Schedule, or Awareness objectives. Age-gate all campaigns to 18+. Health data cannot be used for audience targeting.

Rule of Thumb

If a term describes how someone will feel or what they experience — it's safe. If it describes what a treatment will fix, cure, or diagnose — it's a flag. Swap any medical verb for a wellness verb: support, optimize, enhance, restore.
